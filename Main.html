The Dream of the Red Chamber


One of the Four Great Classical Novels in Chinese Literature.

Overview

Dream of the Red Chamber (Honglou meng) or The Story of the Stone (Shitou ji) is a Chinese novel composed by Cao Xueqin (曹雪芹) in the mid-18th century. One of the Four Great Classical Novels of Chinese literature, it is known for its psychological scope and its observation of the worldview, aesthetics, lifestyles, and social relations of 18th-century China.
